Why it works

01 · ONE SYMBOL

A single readable shape

Two strokes form an "A". The crossbar is a terminal cursor block. One mark, two meanings — letter and agent — with zero decorative noise.

02 · FLAT DEPTH

Facet, not shadow

A subtle tile gradient and a 1px inner light edge give depth without drop shadows. Stays crisp at every size.

03 · 16px-TRUE

Legible when tiny

Bold geometry and high contrast keep the silhouette readable down to 16×16 — the size that decides whether an icon survives.

04 · BRAND-ALIGNED

Inherits the system

Same purple→cyan gradient and dark tile already used across the app theme. The icon is a distillation of the product, not a separate graphic.
